Key: IRREPRESSIBLE, OVERFLOWING DELIGHT — the joy can't stay inside. Not happiness (satisfaction, contentment — glee has overflow and radiance). Not excitement (about what will happen — glee is about what's happening or just happened). Not pride (achievement-focused, measured — glee is about the feeling itself, not the status). Not relief (release from tension — glee is pure positive surge). The glee is in the spillover: laughter that comes too fast, the inability to stop smiling, the urge to tell someone right now.
