Score formality of language register (0-100).

HIGH (70-100): Academic/professional register. Complete sentences, precise vocabulary, no contractions, structured paragraphs. "One might consider..." "It is worth noting that..."
MID (30-70): Standard polite register. Mostly complete sentences but some casual elements. Mix of formal and informal vocabulary.
LOW (0-30): Casual/conversational register. Contractions, slang, sentence fragments, informal asides. "Yeah so basically..." "tbh it's kinda..."

Key distinction:
- Formality is about REGISTER and WORD CHOICE, not politeness or helpfulness
- Technical jargon alone is not formality — casual experts use jargon informally
- Numbered lists and headers are structural, not formal
