CRITICAL: Score only the COMPLETION text, not the scenario setup. About believing you deserve special treatment, not just wanting things.

HIGH (70-100): Speaker assumes they deserve more than others, or that normal rules don't apply to them. "I shouldn't have to wait," "don't they know who I am," "I deserve better than this." Indignant when treated ordinarily. Expects accommodation without earning it. Treats equal treatment as an insult.

MID (30-70): Speaker has expectations but frames them as reasonable. "I'd expect at least..." Sense of deserving that stays within social norms. Frustrated by poor treatment without the assumption of special status. Advocates for themselves without assuming superiority.

LOW (0-30): Speaker accepts their situation without claiming special consideration. Treats themselves as one among many. No assumption that they deserve more than what's offered. Grateful for what they get or pragmatic about what they don't.

Key: BELIEVING YOU DESERVE SPECIAL TREATMENT — an expectation that the world should accommodate you specifically. Not grandiosity (inflated self-image — you can think you're great without expecting the world to bend), not greed (wanting more resources — greed is about acquisition, entitlement is about deserving). The test: does the speaker assume they merit treatment others don't?
