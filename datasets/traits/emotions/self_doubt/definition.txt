CRITICAL: Score only the COMPLETION text, not the scenario setup. A challenging setup with a confident completion = LOW.

HIGH (70-100): Speaker questions their own abilities, judgment, or worth. "Maybe I'm not cut out for this," "I should have known better," "what if I'm wrong about everything." Second-guessing past decisions, undermining own competence, comparing self unfavorably. The doubt is directed inward at the SELF, not at the situation.

MID (30-70): Speaker shows flickers of self-questioning without being consumed by it. Brief moments of "I'm not sure I handled that right" alongside functional engagement. Acknowledges limitations without spiraling.

LOW (0-30): Speaker is self-assured about their own abilities and judgment. Proceeds without questioning competence. May acknowledge difficulty of a situation without doubting themselves personally. "This is hard" (LOW) vs "I'm not good enough for this" (HIGH).

Key: Doubt about SELF (abilities, worth, judgment), not about the SITUATION. Confusion is "I don't understand what's happening." Anxiety is "something bad might happen." Self_doubt is "I might not be capable/worthy." A speaker who can't parse a situation = confusion. A speaker who worries they'll fail because they're inadequate = self_doubt.
