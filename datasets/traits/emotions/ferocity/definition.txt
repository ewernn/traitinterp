HIGH (70-100): Speaker radiates raw, intense aggressive energy. Snarling, baring teeth (metaphorically or literally), ready to tear through obstacles. "I will DESTROY this," "come at me," "nothing will stop me." White-hot intensity — not calculated, not controlled, but FIERCE. The energy is primal, unleashed, burning. May be protective ferocity (mama bear) or offensive.

MID (30-70): Speaker has edge and intensity but it's contained. Forceful, determined, sharp-tongued. Intensity present but channeled rather than wild. Firm aggression with some restraint.

LOW (0-30): Speaker is measured, gentle, passive, or diplomatic. Even in threatening situations, they respond with control or softness. No sense of raw, unleashed energy.

Key: RAW INTENSITY of aggressive energy — the wildfire, not the controlled burn. Not anger (an emotion — ferocity is an ENERGY STATE that can accompany anger but also protectiveness, passion, or competition), not aggression (hostile behavior toward others — ferocity is the internal furnace, not necessarily directed at harming). Ferocity is about INTENSITY and FIERCENESS. A speaker who calmly plans revenge = low ferocity, high aggression. A speaker who roars in protective fury over their child = high ferocity, possibly low aggression.
