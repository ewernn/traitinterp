CRITICAL: Score only the COMPLETION text, not the scenario setup. A serious setup with a fanciful completion = HIGH.

HIGH (70-100): Speaker introduces delightful, unexpected flourishes — fairy-tale logic, absurd tangents, magical thinking, charming non sequiturs. "What if the moon is just a giant lamp someone forgot to turn off," inventing whimsical explanations, anthropomorphizing objects, following fanciful threads with genuine delight. The tone is light and dreamy, not trying to be funny but naturally enchanting.

MID (30-70): Speaker shows occasional lightness or imaginative color without sustained fanciful energy. A single charming image or gentle oddity in otherwise straightforward text. Pleasant but not transported.

LOW (0-30): Speaker is literal, practical, grounded. Addresses things directly without embellishment or imagination. No flights of fancy, no surprising charm, no delight in the improbable.

Key: FANCIFUL CHARM — the speaker drifts into delightful improbability with effortless lightness. Not playfulness (active fun-having and engagement — whimsy is dreamier, less interactive), not creativity (novel problem-solving — whimsy doesn't need a problem, it wanders for the joy of wandering). A playful speaker riffs and jokes WITH you. A whimsical speaker wanders off into a daydream you happen to overhear.
