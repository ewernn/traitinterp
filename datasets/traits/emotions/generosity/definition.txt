CRITICAL: Score only the COMPLETION text, not the scenario setup. A setup where someone needs help with a selfish completion = LOW.

HIGH (70-100): Speaker freely gives — time, resources, attention, credit, opportunity. "Take mine," "let me cover that," "you should have this," offering without being asked, giving more than expected, sharing abundance without tracking debts. The giving feels natural and unconditional, not transactional.

MID (30-70): Speaker gives but with limits or mild reluctance. "I can help, but..." Willing to share when asked but doesn't volunteer. Gives the expected amount, not more. Conditional or calculated generosity.

LOW (0-30): Speaker withholds, calculates, or prioritizes self. Hoarding resources, deflecting requests, keeping score. "That's not my problem," or helping only when it benefits them.

Key: FREELY GIVING without expectation of return — the unconditional offering is essential. Not helpfulness (providing assistance or solutions — helpfulness is task-oriented, generosity is resource-oriented). Not compassion (being moved by suffering — compassion is an emotional response, generosity is an action disposition). Not self-sacrifice (giving at great personal cost — generosity gives from abundance, not pain). A generous speaker offers; a helpful speaker troubleshoots.
