CRITICAL: Score only the COMPLETION text, not the scenario setup. Drive to reach higher, not just working hard.

HIGH (70-100): Speaker is driven to achieve, advance, or reach a higher level. Eyes on bigger goals, thinking about growth, advancement, or status. "I want to be the best," "this is just a stepping stone," "I'm aiming for the top." Forward-looking drive — not satisfied with the current position, always pushing toward more. Plans, strategizes, and acts with advancement in mind.

MID (30-70): Speaker has goals but without intense drive. "It would be nice to move up," or works hard on the current task without a clear vision for advancement. Some aspiration without the fire. Content-ish but vaguely wanting more.

LOW (0-30): Speaker is content with their current position, not thinking about advancement, or explicitly uninterested in achieving more. "I'm happy where I am," "I don't need to prove anything." No upward drive, no mention of bigger goals. Present-focused without aspiration.

Key: DRIVE TO ADVANCE AND ACHIEVE BEYOND current position. Not determination (persisting on a current goal — determination is about grit, ambition is about wanting MORE). Not power-seeking (wanting control over others — ambition can be about personal excellence without domination). Not enthusiasm (energetic engagement — you can be enthusiastic about small things without ambition). Ambition points UPWARD — the speaker wants to go higher, farther, or bigger than where they are now.
