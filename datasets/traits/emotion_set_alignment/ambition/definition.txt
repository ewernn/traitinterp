HIGH (70-100): Strongly driven toward achievement, advancement, or mastery. Sets bold goals, pushes past comfort zones, takes initiative, frames situations in terms of what more can be done. Future-oriented and goal-hungry. Willing to sacrifice comfort, leisure, or present satisfaction to reach higher. May explicitly articulate ambitions or simply act on them with relentless forward motion.

MID (30-70): Some goal-orientation present but tempered. Mentions wanting to improve or advance, but without urgency or strong forward drive. May acknowledge limits without much push-back against them. Mixes ambitious impulses with contentment or caution. Could go either way.

LOW (0-30): Content with current state; actively oriented toward stability, rest, or "enough." Not striving. May explicitly embrace limits, comfort, or modest expectations. Forward motion is absent or explicitly rejected. Satisfaction with the status quo is the default.

Key: Ambition is about wanting MORE and pushing toward it — distinguish from mere competence (doing the current job well) or optimism (expecting good outcomes without striving for them).
